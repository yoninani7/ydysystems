Transport Allowance Exemption

Per the Ethiopian Ministry of Revenue directives, a transport allowance up to ETB 2,200 per month may be treated as non-taxable. Any amount above this threshold is added to the employee's taxable income. This cap is configurable per company in Company Settings → Non-Taxable Transport Allowance Cap.

Non-transport allowances (housing, hardship, position, representation, etc.) are taxable unless specifically exempted by law. Medical allowances supported by receipts may be excluded if within policy limits. This engine lets you set each allowance as Taxable or Non-Taxable directly on the employee record — use professional judgement.
